Did you know that a Panda's Scientific name is Ailuropoda Melanoleuca which means "black and white cat foot".

A Panda's Lifespan is 15-20 years in the wild.

Panda's eat bamboo which are evergreen perennial flowering plants.

Pandas adult weight is 165-353 pounds.

A Panda's weight at birth is 4 to 8 ounces.

Pandas eat 25 to 40 pounds of bamboo per day.

A Panda's adult body length is 4 to 5 feet.

With just over 1,000 left in the world, giant panda's are on the brink of exstinction.

Known as a Panda Bear Habitat: Southwestern China.

Giant panda is sometimes used to distinguished it from the red panda.

Trophic Level is Herbivorous which means panda's are adapted to eating plant material.

Pandas actually have the digestive system of a carnivore,but have adapted to a vegetarian diet.
